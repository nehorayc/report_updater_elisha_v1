# Artificial Intelligence Progress Report (2023)

## Introduction
Artificial Intelligence has seen a massive surge in popularity following the release of ChatGPT in late 2022. This report summarizes the state of the industry as of mid-2023.

## Large Language Models
As of early 2023, GPT-4 is the state-of-the-art model in the industry. Other players like Google have released Bard, and Meta has released Llama 1 for research purposes. The focus is currently on scale and increasing parameter counts.

## AI Hardware and Datacenters
The demand for H100 GPUs from NVIDIA is at an all-time high. In 2023, datacenters are being rapidly expanded to handle the training requirements of massive models. Most of the energy consumption concerns are based on data from the 2020-2022 period.

## AI Regulation
Governments are beginning to discuss the AI Act in Europe. In the US, there are initial hearings regarding the safety of AI models, but no major federal legislation has been passed as of June 2023.

## Conclusion
The AI field is moving fast, and 2023 marks the beginning of the generative AI era.
